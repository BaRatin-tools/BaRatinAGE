Training

Question: are there training sessions on BaRatinAGE?

Answer: yes, several options are available. We can organise on request sessions dedicated to BaRatinAGE, typically during 2 days. For more information on these "baratinades" contact us!

---

License, source codes, etc.

Question: can I distribute BaRatinAGE to all my colleagues, or on my website?

Answer: no, because the BaRatinAGE license is an individual license. We therefore ask each user to send an email saying they accept the terms of the free license.

---

Question: can I have access to the source code of BaRatinAGE?

Answer: BaRatinAGE license is not open source because we use a Fortran library that is not. We hope to eventually solve this problem and migrate to an open-source license. In the meantime you can send us your query and we will try to answer.
